9999px spacing: unit: 8px container-max-width: 1200px gutter: 24px margin-mobile: 20px margin-desktop: 40px section-padding: 80px --- ## Brand & Style The brand identity of the design system is rooted in the philosophy of "Invisible Design"—where the interface recedes to let the product photography and content take center stage. It targets a discerning audience that values quality, clarity, and ease of use. The emotional response is one of calm confidence and high-end reliability. The design style is **Modern Minimalism**. It utilizes expansive whitespace to create a sense of luxury and focus. The aesthetic is characterized by extreme typographic precision, a restrained color palette, and a "product-as-hero" layout strategy. Elements are treated as physical objects in a virtual space, emphasizing tactile responsiveness and structural integrity without unnecessary ornamentation. ## Colors The color strategy for this design system is monochromatic with a singular functional accent. - **Primary & Text:** `#1D1D1F` is used for all primary headings and body text to provide a softer, more premium contrast than pure black. - **Accent:** `#0066CC` is reserved strictly for interactive elements like links, primary buttons, and selection states. - **Backgrounds:** The interface relies on a tiered neutral system. `#F5F5F7` serves as the global canvas, while `#FFFFFF` is used for elevated components (cards, modals) to create depth through color rather than heavy shadows. - **Execution:** Avoid gradients. Use solid fills to maintain the "editorial" feel of the interface. ## Typography This design system uses a dual-font approach to replicate the "Display" and "Text" nuances of premium Swiss-style typography. **Hanken Grotesk** is used for headlines and display text, featuring tighter tracking and higher weights to create impact. **Inter** is used for all body text and UI labels, chosen for its exceptional legibility and systematic feel. Typography should be treated with strict hierarchy. Large display type should use negative letter-spacing (`-0.01em` to `-0.02em`) to appear more cohesive, while small labels benefit from slight tracking (`0.02em`) to ensure readability. ## Layout & Spacing The layout follows a **Fixed Grid** model for desktop and a **Fluid Grid** for mobile. - **Grid:** A 12-column grid is used for desktop (max-width: 1200px) to maintain a focused, readable content area. On mobile, a 4-column fluid grid is used. - **Rhythm:** Spacing follows an 8px baseline. Use 80px or 120px of vertical padding between major sections to emphasize the minimalist aesthetic. - **Alignment:** Content is generally centered in the viewport. Within cards or modules, use 24px or 32px of internal padding to prevent crowding. - **Reflow:** On mobile, margins reduce to 20px, and multi-column grids collapse into a single vertical stack. ## Elevation & Depth Depth is primarily communicated through **Tonal Layers** rather than shadows. This creates a flat, sophisticated look that feels "printed" onto the screen. 1. **Level 0 (Base):** The neutral background (`#F5F5F7`). 2. **Level 1 (Surface):** Elevated components like cards and input fields use pure white (`#FFFFFF`) against the neutral background. 3. **Level 2 (Interaction/Navigation):** The navigation bar uses a **Glassmorphism** effect (80% opacity white with a 20px backdrop blur) to provide context of the content beneath. 4. **Shadows:** When necessary for modals or menus, use an "Ambient Shadow"—a very soft, large-radius blur (30px-50px) with low opacity (5-8%) and no spread. ## Shapes The shape language is consistently **Rounded**, conveying a friendly yet precise feel. - **Standard Elements:** Buttons, small inputs, and chips use a `0.5rem` (8px) radius. - **Containers:** Large cards and product modules use a `rounded-xl` (1.5rem / 24px) or higher radius to create a distinct "object" look. - **Media:** Product imagery should always follow the container's corner radius for a nested, cohesive appearance. ## Components - **Buttons:** Primary buttons are either pill-shaped or 12px rounded rectangles. Use `#0066CC` for primary actions and `#F5F5F7` with `#1D1D1F` text for secondary actions. - **Cards:** White backgrounds (`#FFFFFF`) on the gray canvas (`#F5F5F7`). Cards should have no border; depth is created solely by the color contrast. - **Input Fields:** Use a subtle background fill of `#FFFFFF` with a 1px border of `#D2D2D7` that darkens on focus. - **Chips/Badges:** Small, pill-shaped elements with a subtle gray fill and dark text. Used for categories or product tags. - **Lists:** Clean, edge-to-edge layout with 1px horizontal dividers in a light gray. - **Navigation:** A persistent top-bar with a frosted glass effect and thin 1px bottom border. - **Interaction:** All hover states should be subtle, such as a slight dimming of the background color or a micro-scale increase of a card.
